AutoBE - Background Music Credit
================================

The optional background music feature can use royalty-free / non-copyright
lofi music. If you use background music in AutoBE, please credit the source.

Official credited track for AutoBE background music:
  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XCFz3Xwx70Q

Place a converted audio file (e.g. background.ogg or lofi.mp3) in the
"music" folder next to AutoBE and enable "Play background music" in Settings.
